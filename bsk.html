WELCOME TO BSKPOWERPLUS LIMITED

BSK POWER PLUS ZAMBIA LIMITED is a Zambian registered company fully managed and operated by Zambians, the Company was registered and incorporated with the registrar of companies in 2009. BSK POWER PLUS is purely a mechanical maintenance and servicing company comprising a breed of highly skilled and committed labour force. We enjoy the use of very competitive high tech pieces of mechanical utility items for alignments, balancing and body refurbishment. BSK POWER PLUS ZAMBIA LIMITED enjoys cordial business cooperate relationship with a wide range of mining companies on the Copperbelt Province of Zambia. The company further extends its services to both Government and the private social community.
